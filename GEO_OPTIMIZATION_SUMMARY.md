## ðŸŽ“ Key Takeaways

1. **GEO is Not SEO**: While SEO still matters, GEO focuses on being the cited answer, not just ranking
2. **Answer-First Wins**: AI engines prefer immediate, direct answers with concrete data
3. **Structured Data is Critical**: Schema.org markup helps AI understand and cite content accurately
4. **Off-Site Authority Matters**: Citations from Reddit, LinkedIn, Quora build AI trust
5. **Speed + Text-First**: Fast loading with text content available before JS improves AI crawling
6. **Confidence & Authority**: Factual, definitive tone gets more AI citations than tentative language
7. **Quotable Metrics**: Concrete numbers (14 days, 9,000 pincodes, 99% SLA) are highly citable
8. **Indian Context Matters**: DISHA Act, IT Act 2000, ABDM compliance are semantic signals for Indian healthcare
